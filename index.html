About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management (ISEM) at the National University of Singapore (NUS), starting from August 2025, advised by Prof. WANG Tong.

Prior to this, I completed my MSc in Data-Driven Modeling at HKUST (GPA 3.96/4.3) and worked as a Research Assistant in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics (IEDA) under Prof. ZHAN Ruohan.

My research interests include:

  • Reinforcement learning
  • Supply chain optimization
