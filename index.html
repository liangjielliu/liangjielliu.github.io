About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management (ISEM) at the National University of Singapore (NUS), starting from August 2025. Prior to this, I completed my MSc in Data-Driven Modeling and worked as a Research Assistant at the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics (IEDA) at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

During my time at HKUST, I also interned as a Research Data Scientist at Tencent’s Weixin Group, where I studied how recommendation interference affects user retention, focusing on causal inference, experimental design, and modeling user behavior.

From 2023 to 2024, I worked full-time as a software engineer at the Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences, developing database drivers, APIs, and SQL-based tools. I earned my B.Sc. in Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2023.

My current research interests focus on data-driven decision making, reinforcement learning and supply chain optimization, aiming to improve decision-making in uncertain and dynamic environments.
